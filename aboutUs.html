About Us

History

Founded in 2005 by Master Hiroshi Tanaka, DoBu Gym has a rich history deeply rooted in a profound love for martial arts and a holistic approach to well-being.

What began as a modest martial arts studio has blossomed into a thriving community hub, offering a diverse range of classes and cutting-edge facilities. Master Tanaka's vision was not just to create a space for physical training but to establish a community where individuals could find strength, discipline, and a pathway to a healthy lifestyle.
